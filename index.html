BIOGRAPHY

Hello! My name is Maudiel Alexander Romero and I am a junior at Rutgers University, where I am pursuing a Bachelors degree in Computer Science. Currently, I have an Associates Degree in Computer Science from Bergen Community College (go Bulldogs!). I also have a number of non-academic pursuits and interests that fulfill my passions and social responsibility. For instance, I am the president of the Queer and Christian Club (the QUAC) and also the treasurer for the Out in Tech club (OiTRU). These clubs work in earnest to ensure a safe space for the LGBTQ+ community, and I am very proud to be an integral part of both groups. The main purpose of the OiTRU e-Board is to give computer science and IT students hands-on experience by redesigning websites for local businesses. As for career goals, I hope to enter the field of full stack proggramer as a well-rounded developer. If you have any questions, please shoot me an email through the icon above.
